Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Hygifec products and services.

Yes, all Hygifec products are formulated with safe, non-toxic ingredients that are gentle yet effective. We avoid harsh chemicals and use eco-friendly alternatives that are safe for your entire family, including children and pets. However, as with any cleaning product, we recommend keeping them out of reach of children and pets, and following the usage instructions on the label.

Our products typically have a shelf life of 24 months when unopened. Once opened, we recommend using them within 12 months for optimal effectiveness. Each product comes with a batch number and expiry date printed on the packaging. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to maximize shelf life.

Yes, we offer special bulk pricing for businesses, hotels, and other commercial customers. Please contact our sales team through the contact form or email us at business@hygifec.com for bulk order inquiries. We can provide customized quotes based on your requirements and volume.

We offer free shipping on orders over $50 within the continental United States. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. For orders under $50, a flat shipping rate of $5.99 applies. International shipping is available to select countries, with rates calculated at checkout based on destination and order weight.

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all our products. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return unopened products for a full refund. For opened products, we offer store credit or exchange. Contact our customer service team within 30 days of purchase to initiate a return. Please note that shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the product is defective.

Yes, environmental sustainability is a core value at Hygifec. Our products are biodegradable and packaged in recyclable containers. We use plant-based ingredients wherever possible and avoid harmful chemicals that can damage ecosystems. Our manufacturing processes are designed to minimize water waste and energy consumption, and we're constantly working to reduce our environmental footprint.

No, Hygifec is proudly cruelty-free. We never test our products on animals, nor do we work with suppliers who conduct animal testing. Our products are tested using alternative methods and human volunteers to ensure safety and effectiveness while maintaining our commitment to ethical business practices.
